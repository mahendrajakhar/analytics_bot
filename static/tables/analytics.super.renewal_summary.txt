Table: analytics.super.renewal_summary
Description: Comprehensive tracking of subscription renewals, user engagement metrics, and cancellation patterns across different platforms, languages, and subscription types. This table helps analyze factors affecting renewal rates and churn.
Business teams likely use this to optimize renewal rates, reduce cancellations, and identify engagement thresholds that correlate with higher retention.

primary key: A composite key of `date_tz`, `monetization_platform`, `plan_id`, and `payment_gateway` would likely create uniqueness, possibly with additional dimensions like `monetization_language` and `user_age`.

Key Metrics and Business Usage
